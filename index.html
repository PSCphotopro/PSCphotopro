Photograph Example

  • The photograph should clearly show the shoulders and face, with the face being clear and legible.
  • The user should fill in their name as it appears on their Kerala PSC profile and include the date the photo was taken.
  • The background should be light and clean.

Autograph Example

  • The autograph should be signed in blue or black ink.
  • Ensure the signature is legible and matches the name provided in the application.
  • The signature should be placed within the designated space and not overlap with other elements.
